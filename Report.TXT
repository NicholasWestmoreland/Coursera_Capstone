Introduction/business problem

A car manifacturer based in seattle in looking to improve or include certain safty features
in its vehicles and wants to look at the factors and conditions particular to the city that constibute to 
collisions. 

Historical collision data collected in the city could be of great use here as
understanding what factors are the major constributers in the severity of accidents 
would provide a starting point in determining which features need to be included or improved.
This would help to reduce road deaths, property damage and costs for the city, as well as
improving the car manifacturer image.
